What's Lisk?

Lisk is a terminal app for macOS and Linux (and Windows, of course!) to remember your passwords so you don't have to.

You can generate secure passwords and save them to your Lisk database. Strong passwords are important so it's harder for hackers to hack into your account and steal all your personal information.
